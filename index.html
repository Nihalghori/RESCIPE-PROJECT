BEEF BRGER

INGREDIENTS

  • Egg. Adding an egg to every pound of beef improves both the consistency and the flavor, and keeps it from falling apart on the grill. ...
  • 100ml milk
  • Bacon. ...
  • Onions
  • Bread Crumbs.
  • Worcestershire sauce. ..
  • Garlic.
  • Grated or shredded cheese.

RECIPE

STEP 1:
In a bowl, mix ground beef, egg, onion, bread crumbs, Worcestershire, garlic, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper until well blended.
Divide mixture into four equal portions and shape each into a patty about 4 inches wide
STEP 2:
Lay burgers on an oiled barbecue grill over
a solid bed of hot coals or high heat on a gas grill (you can hold your hand at grill level only 2 to 3 seconds);
STEP 3:
Lay buns, cut side down, on grill and cook until lightly toasted, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
STEP 4:
Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on bun bottoms. Add lettuce, tomato, burger, onion, and salt and pepper to taste. Set bun tops in place
